Key principles
Progressive overload
Add weight in small increments — 1–2.5 kg — to keep muscles adapting without joint strain.
Protein intake
Aim for 1.6–2.2 g of protein per kg of bodyweight daily to maximize muscle protein synthesis.
Rep range
5–10 reps at challenging load for hypertrophy. Keep form strict — weight amplifies bad mechanics.
Recovery
48–72 hours between sessions per muscle group. Sleep 7–9 hrs — growth happens at rest.
Skill balance
Mix weighted sessions with bodyweight skill work (planche, front lever) to stay functional.
Deload weeks
Every 4–6 weeks, reduce load by 40% for one week. Prevents overtraining and boosts long-term gains.
